Volunteers

Explore volunteering opportunities with us

From raising money working in our charity shops to helping our rehoming centres as a dog walker, gardener, or foster carer, - there are plenty of fetching volunteering roles to get involved with, have fun, and help our four-legged friends to live their best lives.

Becoming a Volunteer

We welcome volunteering applications from all backgrounds but will be sure to list any specific requirements needed in the role descriptions. We will support you with any training you need to get the most out of your time with us. While it varies from role to role, our rehoming centre or shop volunteers typically contribute up to three hours a week, but we are grateful for any time you can commit and would love to hear from you. Please note you must be at least 18 years old to volunteer with us.

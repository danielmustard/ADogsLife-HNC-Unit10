A Dogs Life - Kennel Service

We are your local dog home boarding service giving you complete peace of mind when you can’t take Fido with you. Our family of dog holiday experts provide luxury pet care in our experienced hosts’ cosy homes. Barking Mad was established in 2000 as the original alternative to kennels. Our professional dog sitting service works by meeting you in person for bespoke care because we believe dogs deserve a holiday too!

Our Kennel Service


Full Day Service

We have plenty of space with both an indoor and outdoor area. The area is completely safe and secure so you can rest, assured that they’re in the best hands.

Half Day Care

We offer half day doggy care both in the mornings and afternoons. Please contact us in advance for availability to book for a morning or afternoon session.

Dog Training

We can offer full dog training services and classes through our dog trainer.
